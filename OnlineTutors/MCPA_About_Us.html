About Us

OTPA was established in 2020 and is a platform build to empower students. It is a platform that was built by students for students, with the goal of helping those who are struggling to find suitable tutors that will work well with them and understand their needs. This platform was in idea of none other than our very own Sincanyiwe Ntuli, who proposed the idea to the rest of the team, who took heed and decided to take action. The results? OTPA was born.
